About Us

A hospital the community trusts


Life Line Hospital is a trusted multi-specialty hospital at 1B1, Pallempapiah Street, Ganagapeta, Kadapa — just 0.68 km from Cuddapah Railway Station, near the famous 7 Roads Circle.

We provide round-the-clock emergency care, specialist consultations, advanced diagnostics, and surgical services under one roof. Our team is led by Dr. Shaik Rabbani, one of the most highly regarded physicians in Kadapa.
