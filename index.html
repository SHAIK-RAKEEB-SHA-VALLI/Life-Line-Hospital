About

Life Line Multi Specialty Hospital


At Life Line Multi Specialty Hospital, we believe that healthcare is not just about treating disease — it's about healing people.

Established to serve YSR Kadapa District, we are an 80-bedded advanced multispeciality hospital equipped with modern technology and a team of experienced doctors who put patients first.

From emergency to recovery, from surgery to dialysis, our mission is simple: Quality treatment, Compassionate care, Affordable for all.

Because when you are in safe hands, healing begins faster.

  • ✓80-bed multi-specialty hospital with advanced ICU
  • ✓Open 24 hours, 7 days including all public holidays
  • ✓Dialysis Centre — coming soon via PPP model
  • ✓PM-JAY & Arogya Sri government scheme accepted
  • ✓Emergency trauma care with rapid response team
